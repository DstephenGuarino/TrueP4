  1. Where was the Mothman sighted?
  • Mothman is commonly associated with the state of west virginia. This is due to the bulk of the original Mothman sightings taking place in west virginia, typically in an area designated the tnt zone. while this is the sight most commonly associated with the creature mothman has bee seen around the world and often times appears before disaster.
  1. What does the mothman look like?
  • Mothman is typically described as a tall humanoid figure, covered in dark feathers, with large powerful wings. Mothman is typically depicted as being in a hunched over position creating the illusion of no head. Mothman's most notable feature is its distinct large read eyes that are said to give off an eerie glow.
  1. What is the most important Mothman sighting?
  • The most important sighting of Mothman is most likely the lover's report from 1966. In this report on November 15, 1966 2 couples(Roger and Linda Scarberry, and Steve and Mary Mallette) reported seeing a "large flying man with ten foot wings" and eyes that "glowed red". The story goes that the two couples where leaving a popular spot for couples outside of Point Pleasant. On their return trip they drove through an area known as the "TNT area" which was a former World War II munitions plant. While driving they passed by what they initially believed to be a regular human. This "human" stretched out its wings and began to chase their vehicle. To there horror accelerating their car did not help as the creature was able to keep pace with their car at speeds of "about 100 miles an hour‚Äù. After driving for a short period of time the vehicle left the TNT Area and the creature gave up chase. The couples immediately reported their incident to the local sheriff and this kicked off Mothman-hysteria. For about a full year the locals reported various sightings of Mothman associating him with UFO's, Men in black, and blamed him for everything from dog disappearances to tv antennas breaking.
  1. Cultural impact
  • Mothman is a Cryptid that has left a significant impact on pop culture and the people of West Virginia. In terms of pop-culture Mothman has been referenced in a variety of different media, but most recently has appeared as a boss in the video game Fallout 76 as an optional mini boss. For the people of West Virginia the town of Point Pleasant celebrates this creature and the legacy it left in many ways. Some of these ways include, erecting a statue in its honor, having a museum dedicated to telling the tale of Mothman, and hosting an annual Mothman festival with games,rides, and a Mothman pancake eating contest.
  1. How valid is the sighting and is there a reasonable explanation?
  • This website is under the firm belief that the couples sighting is the most valid of many mothman sightings. This has significantly more credibility than other reports as the detective who interviewed the couples was able to verify that the couples were indeed scared and that he knew these people where of good quality character. As for an explanation many believe that an owl or unidentified bird was the real creature behind the mothman. This makes sense as it would explain Mothman's bright glowing eyes, the strange low almost no head, and the large wingspan.
