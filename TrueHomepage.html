What is the purpose of this page?

The purpose of this website is to educate the public about various Cryptids. A Cryptid is typically defined as a creature that doesn't have enough evidence to prove or disprove their existence. These creatures could be something unusual like an unknown species of cat living in an area said to not have large cats, or could be something scientifically unusual like an extinct species or a species that defies known science. While Cryptozoology has led to the discovery of unknown species(the discovery of a special lungless frog) it is often times painted as a Pseudo science due to the fact the more astronomical side of Cryptozoology is exposed to the public. This website would like to inform the public of several, different Cryptids and teach them not only of various creatures supposedly seen in the world, but also convince them that even in Cryptozoology's more unbelievable parts, it can still provide an insight into what the human mind is capable of. We also want give the readers a sense of adventure as even if we explored the vast majority of the land, it is possible somethings still allude our grasp.

How should readers take the information they read about Cryptids?

Usually unless proven to exist, or to have once existed, a case could be made both for and against the existence of each Crypid. This Means that is important for all individuals to carefully examine the evidence that they are given and make a conclusion on their own. Taking this mindset will help prevent Cryptozoology from becoming further fictionalised and help individuals from falling for faked evidence. It is also important for individuals to have an open mind as they could be proven wrong, but they may also craft their own theories that turn out to be true.
