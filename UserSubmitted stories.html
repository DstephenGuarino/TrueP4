User submitted stories

What constitutes a User Submitted story?

A user Submitted story is a paranormal or Cryptid encounter submitted to this website by readers around the globe. The stories submitted by the readers will be read and interpreted by an anonymous grader. If the grader feels the story is both interesting and has a significant amount of validity, they may be added to this website with the initial submitters information removed from public view

User submitted story guidelines

A User Submitted story must contain some element of Paranormal activity, if the strange activity has a known explanation it will not be featured. If a story is faked it will not be featured. If a story features extreme profanity it maybe censored or ignored. Even if a story fits all requirements it may not be uploaded to this website. There is no reward, or monetary gain for an individual who uploads their website it is purely to share their strange encounters.
